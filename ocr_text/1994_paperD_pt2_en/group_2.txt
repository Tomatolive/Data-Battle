INSTRUCTIONS TO CANDIDATES
Paper D comprises:
• 10 questions, all to be answered — no choice for candidates;
• an inquiry from a client requiring an answer in the form of a legal opinion.
50% Of the marks available are awarded for the 10 questions, 50% for the legal
advice.
The answers to questions 1 to 10 should be brief and to the point and the candidate must
cite any Articles, Rules or other legal basis relevant to his answer.
In the legal opinion the candidate should demonstrate his professional ability to master a
complex industrial propeny law situation. He must explain any legal consequences of the
situation postulated and preferably cite any Articles, Rules or other legal basis relevant to
his answer.
